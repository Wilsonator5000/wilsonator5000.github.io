Just who is Wilson Gramer?

Wilson Gramer does not write for a job. He writes for a grade.

Wilson is in the seventh grade and strives to do his best in every subject, be it mathematics, science, history, STEM, or writing. He also participates in the Student Council and Boy Scouts, and works on service projects with his friends. In his spare time he likes to read and work on computer programming. He lives with his mother, father, brother, sister, and five pets.

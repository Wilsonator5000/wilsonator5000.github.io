TODAY AT NOON I heard news about

a man who had made history. I was playing POKÉMON GO at the time, and completely ignored the notification on my phone. And so in reality, I didn’t hear news about a man who had made history until I got home from work at around dinnertime. But did you, the reader, go scavenging the internet, down a shady article site, and off the main navigation page (oops) just to read my hopeless story about my phone and my ignorance? Well, I hope so, but PROBABLY NOT. So here comes the real story.

        BOB ODAKOTA BASSAM, or BOB, started out as we all do, a mere boy. I did a quick Google search on his name, and found out that it literally means “Bob who is friendly and likes to smile.” This, quite fortunately, fits his character well, since ever since he was a boy he has curbed his negativity and became a complete optimist. To the extent where people think that his mind is a bit askew. But besides the fact that he was incompetent in the ability to feel anything but pure, complete happiness, he was a pretty great guy.
